Hello and welcome to my homepage on this the

My name is Jeffrey Philippson and I am a British experimental physicist and technology consultant. I obtained my Ph.D. in experimental physics on the Trent-Queen's graduate program in Canada. My research in experimental atomic, molecular and optical physics was carried out as part of the Optical Physics Group at Trent University in Peterborough, Ontario. See my research summary, publications and theses pages for further details.

In February 2012 I returned to the UK to pursue a career as an industrial physicist, moving to Cambridge in May 2012 to begin a technology consultancy position with the PA Consulting Group. My current interests lie in product development, process control and optimisation, and precision measurement systems, and I am discovering new avenues of interest every day!
