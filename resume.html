Profile

A highly motivated researcher with extensive experience in applied optics and electronics and a strong track record in the design, construction and testing of optical, optoelectronic and electronic instrumentation. I completed a Ph.D. in Experimental Physics in January 2012 and am currently employed in the technology consultancy sector.
